Account Lifecycle 3 states

The Account entity represents a billable customer. Accounts follow a simple linear lifecycle from creation through activation to potential cancellation.

Draft Active Canceled

Subscription Lifecycle 6 states

Subscriptions represent the recurring billing relationship between an account and rate plans. They support suspension with resume capability, and can be cancelled from either active or suspended states.

Draft Pending Activation Pending Acceptance Active Suspended Cancelled

Payment Lifecycle 6 states

Payments track the processing pipeline from creation through gateway submission to settlement. Note: The Payment status is separate from the gateway state (NotSubmitted, Submitted, Settled, FailedToSettle).

Draft Processing Processed Posted Error Canceled

Invoice Lifecycle 4 states

This represents the invoice document status, not the payment status of the invoice. Invoices are generated from bill runs or API calls, posted for collection, and may be canceled if needed.

Draft Posted Canceled Error

Order Lifecycle 8 states

Orders are the most complex entity, supporting scheduled execution and reversion. Some states (Scheduled, Executing, Failed) require the Scheduled Orders feature. The Reverted state requires the Single Version Subscription feature.

Draft Pending Scheduled Executing Completed Reverted Cancelled Failed

Amendment Lifecycle 4 states + 9 types

Amendments modify existing subscriptions via the Orders API. They have both a status (lifecycle state) and a type (NewProduct, RemoveProduct, UpdateProduct, ChangePlan, Cancellation, Renewal, TermsAndConditions, OwnerTransfer, Composite).

Draft Pending Activation Pending Acceptance Completed
