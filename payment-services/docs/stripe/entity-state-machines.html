Subscription Lifecycle 8 states

Stripe Subscriptions manage recurring billing for customers. They support trial periods, pause/resume, and various cancellation behaviors. The incomplete states handle payment setup failures, while past_due and unpaid manage collection retry logic.

incomplete incomplete_expired trialing active past_due unpaid paused canceled

PaymentIntent Lifecycle 7 states

PaymentIntents track the lifecycle of a payment from creation to completion. They handle various authentication flows (3D Secure, bank redirects), capture modes (automatic vs manual), and provide clear states for client-side integration.

requires_payment_method requires_confirmation requires_action processing requires_capture succeeded canceled

Invoice Lifecycle 5 states

Stripe Invoices are billing documents that can be sent to customers for payment. They start as drafts, are finalized to open, and transition to terminal states based on payment success, failure, or manual action.

draft open paid uncollectible void
